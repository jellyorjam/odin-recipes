Fancy Pants

Description

The fancy pants is an unforgettable, high class experience in your mouth. Think a hot dog can't be fancy? Think again.

Ingredients

  • 100% Beef Wiener
  • Bun for Wiener
  • Goat Cheese
  • Fig Jam
  • Prosciutto
  • Black Pepper Truffle Honey

Steps

  1. Cook wiener to your liking.
  2. Toast bun to your liking.
  3. Smear goat cheese on bun.
  4. Lightly smear fig jam on bun.
  5. Place wiener on bun with care.
  6. Add prosciutto on top of wiener.
  7. Drizzle black pepper truffle honey to taste.
