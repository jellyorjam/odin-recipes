Glimmer Cheesecake

Description

This cheesecake will change your life. It is so good and fancy that you will never want another cheesecake ever again. After eating this cheesecake, you will look at all other cheesecakes and think, 'it's a shame this cheesecake doesn't have gold on it'. Proceed with caution.

Ingredients

  • NY Cheesecake
  • Edible Pure Gold
  • Flaming Berries

Steps

  1. Bake cheesecake, New York style.
  2. Smother in gold.
  3. Flame the berries and add on top.
